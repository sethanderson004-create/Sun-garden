✎ Skyline tracer —

☀ AR sun view beta

Stand at the spot you want to plant, point the phone at the sky, and watch where the sun travels in June, at the equinox, and in December — behind which trees, over which fence.

Spot check: sweep the phone slowly along the arcs and it counts the hours of direct sun for you — then tells you what would grow there.

Hold the phone upright (portrait). Needs camera + motion access — nothing is uploaded, it all runs on your phone. No compass (desktop)? You can drag to look around. Once running, point the crosshair at the sun and tap ☀ Align — that locks the heading far better than the phone's compass can.
