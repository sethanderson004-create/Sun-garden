2 · Trace your skyline

Stand at the spot you care about, look around, and drag on the panorama to draw the outline of whatever blocks the sky — trees, house, fence. Drag to the bottom to erase. Optionally load a panorama photo (0–360°, left edge facing north) as a tracing guide. Colored arcs show the sun's path across your sky.
